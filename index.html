PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE
Hari Om
Educate children in the right way by instilling in them feelings of self respect and national pride-Swami Chinmayananda Today the role of a school is not only to pursue academic excellence but also to motivate and empower its students to be life long learners and productive members of an ever-changing global society. At Vidya Bharati Chinmaya Vidyalaya , we provide an atmosphere to our students for holistic development by providing student centric teaching environment . Gentle enfoldment of the innate potential of the children is encouraged in order to channelize their potential in the pursuit of human excellence. We impart in Chinmayans the tenet of social consciousness and the capacity to formulate ethical opinions. While tramsmitting enriching and innovative learning experiences, we gently edify them to be prepared to stretch their boundaries further and face new challenges outside their comfort zone. The Vidyalaya is striving incessantly to inculcate strong values through an amalgamation of the value based scholastic and co scholastic activities. We aim at holistic development by practicing a simplistic vision i.e. setting high standards of academic achievements, creating experiential and experimental learning atmosphere and ensuring that students 'walk values' and not merely 'talk values'. Thus, students are impassioned to achieve academic excellence and uphold high ideals to combat testing times and towering challenges. The parent's support strengthens our efforts in moulding the future of children. Their consistent support empowers us to envision lofty ideas and concretize them. I am confident that with the cooperation of students , staff and parents Vidya Bharati Chinmaya Vidyalaya will scale greater heights.
ARISE, AWAKE AND STOP NOT TILL THE GOAL IS REACHED-Swami Vivekananda
